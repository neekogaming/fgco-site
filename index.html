How it works

Three keys. That's the whole loop.

Capture an execution from any input — stick, keyboard or leverless — in training mode, save it to your library, and play it back over your game, frame-perfect, every time.

01 · RECORD

Hit F9 to capture

Start recording with a single hotkey, straight from your input device in the game's training mode. Every input is captured frame by frame.

F9 start / stop recording
02 · SAVE

Name it, tag it, keep it

Save the combo to your local library with a name and tags — yours to organize, browse, and share with a teammate.

F5 open the menu & library
03 · REPLAY

Watch it, or run it

Hit F10 to show the visual guide on-screen, or F1 to auto-play the inputs into the game and let it run the combo for you.

F10 guide F1 auto-play
Built for the lab

Everything the training room is missing.

Always-on-top overlay

A transparent, click-through window sits over your game. No alt-tabbing, no second monitor — your combo stays in your eyeline while you play.

Frame-perfect capture

Combos are recorded directly from your input device — stick, keyboard or leverless — frame by frame, so playback matches exactly what you executed, input for input.

Practice Mode F12

Load a combo and Practice Mode validates your live inputs against it in real time — so you can see exactly where your execution drifts.

Local library

Every combo is saved locally on your machine. Browse and manage your whole collection in the Library — keep them, organize them, or share with a teammate.

Community combos

Browse and download combos contributed by other players directly inside the app — pull a fresh BnB into your library without leaving the overlay.

Rebindable hotkeys

Every hotkey remaps on the Bindings screen, with controller mapping per game. Already using F9, F10, F1, F12 or F5? Move FGCO's keys anywhere that's free.
